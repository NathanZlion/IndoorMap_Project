Introduction Hi there! Im Nathnael, a senior software engineering student at Addis Ababa University (AAU), 5 Kilo campus. After spending more than four years in this campus, I still find it challenging to locate certain offices. If this is my experience, imagine how difficult it must be for visitors or first-year students. This challenge inspired me to think about creating an indoor navigation system that works as seamlessly as Google Maps for outdoor spaces. Thats the foundation of the Indoor Maps project. ...
